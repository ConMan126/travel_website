Scotland - Aviemore

March 2022

Our quick stop in Aviemore was supposed to be just a way to break up a long drive but turned out to be one of the neatest stays of our trip. We enjoyed great hospitality, ample selections of Scotch, and the ambiance of the house was something that can't be recreated. If you ever find yourself in Aviemore, stay at Tigh na Sgiath Country House Hotel, you will not be dissapointed.

March 2022

It's an adventure to just get to the Outer Hebrides as the only way the get here is by ferry! But it was worth it! Truly magical!
